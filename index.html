Welcome to our fundraiser for the Aphasia Community Friendship Center

If you're reading this, someone probably nominated you to play in our fundraiser game. Congrats!

Literally all you have to do is nominate the next person to play. That's it!

Every time someone like you makes a nomination between <date 1> to <date 2>, Backyard Brains donates $5 to support the Aphasia Community Friendship Center (ACFC), a "drop-in" holistic support center for individuals with communication disorders, where participants engage in group speech conversation activities, community music, meditation practice, and art therapy.

Here's How to Nominate the Next Player

  1. You can only nominate the next player if you were nominated to do so.
    If you received a nomination notification over text or email from someone you know, you should already be all set.
  2. Nominate the next player by texting the fundraiser organizer (Michael Loftus) at +1 2484446170.
    He will only need the phone number of your nominee. You can send him a message like this:
    I nominate phone number <replace with your nominee's phone number>
  3. If your nominee has already been nominated to play by someone else, you will need to choose another nominee.
    Michael will let you know if this is the case.
  4. Tell your nominee that you nominated them, and send them this webpage so they know how to play!

If you don't make the next nomination, the fundraiser stops

We're serious! There's only one nominee at a time, and we only raise money when they nominate the next player. If you break the chain by discontinuing the nomination, we won't raise any more funds!
That also means that the slower you are to play, the less money we raise!

Backyard Brains is only donating from <date 1> to <date 2>, so the fewer nominations occur in that timeframe, the less money we make.

We need you to participate!

So what's the catch?

There is none. You don't pay a cent. We're not going to sell any of the player's phone numbers or use them in any other way. We're just trying to find a fun way to raise money for a good cause.
